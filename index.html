Qi-Zhi Xu  (徐其志)

   

    Beijing Institute of Technology

    Email: qizhi@bit.edu.cn

    Office: 5 Zhongguancun South Street, Haidian District, Beijing, China

I am a professor in the School of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering of BIT (北京理工大学).

I was born in Jiangxi Province. In 2005, I got my B.S. degree from Jiangxi Normal University, Nanchang, China. In 2012, I received my Ph.D. degree from Beihang University. I was a Post-Doctoral Fellow with the University of New Brunswick, Fredericton, NB, Canada. My research interest is mainly focusing on image fusion, image understanding, and big data analysis of remote sensing.

 

Space Intelligent Computing Laboratory:

The main research directions include remote sensing image fusion, object detection, change detection and so on.
